Our Story

Where every cup tells a story

Founded in 2024, Coffee day. brings authentic chai traditions from the heart of India together with expertly crafted coffee. Our journey began with a simple belief: that every cup tells a story.

We source our tea leaves directly from family-owned gardens in Darjeeling and our coffee beans from sustainable farms across Colombia and Ethiopia. Every sip supports fair trade practices and environmental sustainability.

Our Mission

At our core, we are passionate about creating a harmonious blend of Asian Chai and European Coffee traditions that celebrates the rich heritage of both cultures. Our mission is to provide an authentic, premium beverage experience that brings people together through the universal language of exceptional tea and coffee. We believe in fostering community connections while honoring traditional brewing methods and introducing innovative flavor profiles that reflect the diverse tastes of our global customers. Through carefully sourced ingredients and expertly crafted beverages, we aim to create moments of comfort, connection, and cultural appreciation in every cup we serve.

Our vision

We envision a world where the ancient art of Asian Chai seamlessly meets the refined tradition of European Coffee, creating a new standard for specialty beverages. Our vision is to establish a network of welcoming spaces that serve as cultural bridges, where the warmth of Asian hospitality meets European sophistication. By 2027, we aspire to be recognized as the premier destination for authentic chai-coffee fusion experiences, known for our commitment to quality, cultural authenticity, and exceptional customer service. We aim to create a brand that not only satisfies taste preferences but also educates and celebrates the beautiful traditions behind each beverage we serve.

Our Journey

Year One Foundation (2025-2026) Our journey begins with establishing 50 carefully selected locations across key metropolitan markets. Each store will serve as a cultural embassy, introducing communities to our unique fusion concept while building a loyal customer base. In year 2 scaling up to 200 locations expanding into secondary locations and expanding to 400 locations in 5 years, with plan of international expansion and continued innovations in offerings.
